Shift space is an interactive online gallery.
In 2020, the relevance of online cultural spaces increases
due to the corona pandemic and its consequences.

We as shift space wanted to rethink the way art is exhibitied online.
But we did not just reproduce the analogue experience.
We intended to develop a real alternative to the everyday gallery
by using the opportunities of digital spaces.

For the following exhibition we curated the works of nine artist
from different fields. We are deeply grateful for their contribution!
Enjoy exploring shift space!
